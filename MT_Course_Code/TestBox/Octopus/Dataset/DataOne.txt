A paragraph is a distinct unit of writing, typically composed of several sentences, that focuses on a single idea or topic. It's a fundamental way to structure and organize written work, making it easier for readers to follow the author's train of thought. Paragraphs help break down complex information into manageable chunks, improving clarity and readability.
